## E-Commerce Customer Behavior & Sales Analysis

This document summarizes key business insights derived from the
data cleaning, exploratory analysis, and customer segmentation
performed in previous days.

---

## Key Business Insights

1. Revenue is concentrated in a small number of product categories,
   indicating clear revenue drivers for the business.

2. A small group of customers contributes a disproportionate share
   of total revenue, highlighting the importance of customer retention.

3. Order values are unevenly distributed, with a few high-value
   purchases influencing overall revenue.

4. Most customers fall into the medium-value segment, representing
   strong potential for upselling and engagement strategies.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Focus marketing and inventory investment on top-performing
   product categories to maximize revenue impact.

2. Implement loyalty programs or personalized offers for high-value
   customers to improve retention.

3. Design targeted promotions for medium-value customers to increase
   purchase frequency and average order value.

4. Re-engage low-value customers through discounts or email campaigns
   to encourage repeat purchases.

---

## Conclusion

This analysis demonstrates how basic data exploration and segmentation
can provide actionable insights that support informed business decisions.
